ABOUT ICONIE 2026

The 5th International Conference on Islamic Economics (ICONIE 2026) is a premier academic event organized by the International Research Centre of Islamic Economics & Finance (IRCIEF), Universiti Islam Selangor (UIS) in collaboration with the Bahrain Institute of Banking and Finance (BIBF).

This conference brings together scholars, researchers, practitioners, and policymakers from around the world to discuss and explore innovative solutions in Islamic economics and finance.

ICONIE 2026 provides an excellent platform for academic exchange, networking, and fostering international collaboration in the field of Islamic economics.

CONFERENCE THEME

Global Debt Crisis and the Islamic Alternatives:
A Roadmap for Just Financial Systems

SUB-THEMES

  • Sovereign Debt
  • Debt Justice and Economic Sovereignty
  • Islamic Social Finance for Fiscal Stability
  • Ethical Public Financing in Theory and Practice
  • Islamic Financial Institutions and Crisis Mitigation
  • Islamic Debt Management Strategies for Countries
  • Profit-and-Loss Sharing Models as Alternative to Interest-Based Lending
  • Debt, Development and the Maqasid al-Shariah
  • The Role of Central Banks and Regulators in Integrating Islamic Finance
  • Islamic Perspectives on IMF, World Bank and Global Financial Institutions
  • Digital Innovations in Islamic Finance for Debt Transparency
  • Case Studies in Islamic Debt Management
